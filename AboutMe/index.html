Introduction

Hello! I'm Mofiyinfoluwa Esan,a fabric vendor and a Fashion Designer with love for creating timeless pieces. I have recently transitioned into tech with AltSchool Africa. Welcome to my website!

Early life and Background

Though a native of Ilesha in Osun State,I was born and raised in the ancient city of Ibadan,Oyo State Nigeria. My dad is a Lawyer while my mum,a Journalist and an Educator. I have three siblings,two brothers and a sister and I am the first child. My family is a closely knit one as not only do I share strong and deep bonds with my siblings but also my cousins. We always met at our grandma's house every on the 1st of January every year which happens to be the first day of the Gregorian calender year. Asides this,there were many weekend get aways also at our grandma's. One of the most interesting parts of being with grandma was the eko and akara(bean cake)/moimoi(beans pudding) which we often had on Friday nights,egbo and ewa on Saturday nights and on Sunday afternoons before all the cousins return to their respective houses she shared a bottle of wine. Interestingly, if any cousin wasn't present,she wouldn't share the wine but keep it till the weekend when everyone came around. On Friday evenings,we all gathered in the sitting room with our mats and matresses to watch wrestling matches which was always shown on the local TV channel alongside granny. Also, as long as granny's eldest son was around,we had frequent eat outs. I always looked forward to this (I too like food!). I am convinced all of these helped build the bond and love that we share.

Education

I began formal education at Davola Premier School, New Bodia,Ibadan after which I proceeded to The International School,Unuversity of Ibadan,Ibadan for my Secondry education between 2004 and 2010. In 2010, I got admitted into the University of Ibadan to study Biochemistry. Although, it was my dream to become a Medical Doctor, I had to settle for Biochemistry inspite of a beautiful Secondary School Certificate Examination(SSCE) result. Medicine and Surgery was higly competitive and priority was give to those who had at least a parent who worked in the University or one of it's affiliated parastatals. I hoped to cross over to Medicine in my second year but alas! In my fourth and final year, I did my project in Nutritional Biochemistry where I carried out a research on the effect of storage time on the nutrient content of homemade tomto paste. I graduated in March 2015 after fulfiling all requirements.

Career

In May 2015, I got mobilised and deployed to Lagos State for my National Youths Service Corps(NYSC). During my service year, I worked in Ok Foods Nigeria Ltd as a member of the Quality Management team. My duty were but not limited to collecting samples of raw materials for production and carrying out specified tests to ensure they meet specifications before they can be accepted. I also compiled results weekly and monthly and sent them to the Quality Assurance Manager via the internet after approval from my supervisor. On completion of my service i was offered employment with the company and had only worked a little while before i was faced with a daunting challenge which forced me to take a career break. A few months down the line I got enrolled in a fashion academy for the duration of 2 years and then I started my Fashion Business, Za's Couture.

Current Endeavours and Future goal

Sometime in January 2024 while having retrospective thinking, I realised how astounding my mum is; constantly improving herself and i thought to myself "this is a standard I shouldn't fall short of". asides this,there's need for me to end the career break I have been on for years. The importance of being in line with the present world trend can also not be over emphasised. Consequently, I decided to speak with a friend who suggested IT and also told me about AltSchool Africa. She directed me to AltSchool's Website to gain more insight and see if it was something I wanted to take on. After reading through, I concluded that the Cloud Engineering track will bring me much gratification. I applied to get enrolled HERE, wrote the assesment and currently enrolled in the Cloud Engineering track with student ID:ALT/SEO/024/0214. At the end of the programme, I hope to have become a world-class Cloud Engineer who can also contribute her own quota to the advancement of technology and also the involvement of women in the tech space. Consequently,I would have another source of income which will provide more capital to successfully run my fashion business. I would also be able to use my knowedge of tech to improve my business.
